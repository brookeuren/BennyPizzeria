About us.

Since our debut, Benny's Pizza has stayed true to its promise of good food, service, and atmosphere. Learn more about our founder, the history of our restaurant, and our mission to our loyal customers.

Who are we?

Our founder, Benny Ricci, believed that all restaurants should achieve three things: to serve great food, to treat customers right, and to create a positive atmosphere where all customers feel right at home. That's why he started Benny's Pizzeria. He hoped to create a restaurant that not only offered amazing food, but also created a place for locals to feel at-ease. Since 2017, we have been dedicated to keeping true to his values, making sure each customer leaves satisfied.
